# PAYROLL_INVENTORY.md – Payroll & Inventory Modules for Clinic IMS This document adds two new modules to the Clinic IMS: **Payroll** (staff compensation, time tracking, leave) and **Inventory** (supplies, vaccines, equipment tracking). Both are accessible only to **Admins** and specific roles with assigned permissions (e.g., Payroll Clerk, Inventory Manager).>**Integration note**: Payroll data links to User accounts (employees).
